The Rising Tide is a Climate Change Interactive Experience. There are many people who disbelieve that Earth's Climate is being altered—even though it is factually proven true.

Carbon dioxide (CO2) is an important heat-trapping (greenhouse) gas, which is released through human activities such as deforestation and burning fossil fuels, as well as natural processes such as respiration and volcanic eruptions.

The Level of Carbon Dioxide in the air is measured in Parts per Million. The Earth should stay at 350ppm(parts per million). December 2016, it was recorded at an all time high of 404ppm.

THE EVIDENCE

The Earth's climate has changed throughout history. Most of these climate changes are attributed to very small variations in Earth’s orbit that change the amount of solar energy our planet receives.

The current warming trend is of particular significance, because it is human-induced. And proceeding at a rate that is unusual in the past 1,300 years.
